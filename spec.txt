Player Statistics



HP (0-100) - Health points remaining

SP (0-100) - Spirit points remaining

SAN (0-100) - Sanity remaining

NRG (0-100) - Energy remaining

LVL (0-100) - player level

XP  ((aaa of bbb) current level dependant) - experience in the current level
